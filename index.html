Book-era


Welcome to Book Era! Immerse yourself in the world of literature and embark on a captivating journey through the realms of knowledge and imagination. This sleek and intuitive Flutter application invites you to explore a vast library of books spanning multiple genres. Dive into rich narratives, discover insightful details about authors and their works, and track your reading progress with ease. Book Era is your gateway to a world of literary exploration and enlightenment.

Features


  • User Authentication: Users can log in to their personal accounts securely.
  • Book Search: Search for books by genre and explore a vast library of titles.
  • Book Details: Access detailed information about books, including summaries, reviews, and author information.
  • Reading Progress: Track your reading progress for each book and set reading goals.
  • Intuitive Design: Enjoy a sleek and user-friendly interface for seamless navigation.

Cosmos: A Space Odyssey


Welcome to Cosmos: A Space Odyssey! Explore the wonders of the universe and embark on an intergalactic journey through more than 20 mesmerizing galaxies. This Flutter application is designed to ignite your curiosity about the cosmos and unravel the mysteries of outer space.

Features


  • Galaxy Search: Dive into a vast collection of over 20 galaxies and discover their unique characteristics, stunning visuals, and intriguing facts.
  • Interactive Interface: Immerse yourself in a user-friendly interface that offers seamless navigation and captivating visuals, making your cosmic exploration an unforgettable experience.
  • Informative Content: Gain insights into each galaxy's composition, size, distance from Earth, and other fascinating details curated by astronomers and space enthusiasts.
